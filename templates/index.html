Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to examine the most common words in three U.S. Speeches. The speeches will be the Gettysburg Address, I have a Dream Speech and finally Einenhower's Military Industrial speech.

Each speech will be displayed on its seperate page. Then the data section will contain graphs which will show the most common words in each speech. Finally, at the end of the graphs, will be a few remarks where I will conclude what, if any, are the common themes.

Finally, I will not be counting articles, words such as the, or and's, or's, but's I think you get the point. I am looking for words that convey an idea across time.
